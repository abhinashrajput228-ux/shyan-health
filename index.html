What is the Keto Diet?

The ketogenic diet is a low-carbohydrate, high-fat eating plan that shifts your body into ketosis. Standard distribution is 75% fat, 20% protein, and 5% carbs.

🔥 Rapid Weight Loss

Appetite suppression and fat burning lead to significant results.

🧠 Brain Function

Ketones provide stable energy, potentially improving mental clarity.

Intermittent Fasting Protocol

The science of cycling between eating and fasting windows

Popular Methods

  • 16/8 Method: 16 hours fasting, 8 hours eating. Most sustainable.
  • 5:2 Diet: Normal eating for 5 days, 500 cals for 2 days.
  • OMAD: One Meal A Day. Advanced technique.

⚖️ Metabolic Health

Lower insulin levels and improved fat access.

🧬 Cellular Repair

Autophagy removes damaged cells and regenerates tissues.

The Carnivore Diet Explained

The ultimate elimination diet: Animal products only

What to Eat

Consists entirely of meat, fish, eggs, and optionally dairy. Removes all plant foods entirely.
